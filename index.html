GHL #1 - Missed Call Text-Back

How it works: Receives a missed-call webhook, immediately sends a text: "Sorry we missed your call! How can we help?" If no reply in 2 hours, sends a second follow-up. After 24 hours of silence, sends a final message with a booking link. Logs everything to Excel.

How it helps: Recovers lost callers who would otherwise go to a competitor. Over 60% of missed-call text-backs get a response — turning missed calls into booked appointments.

Sample Project Automation

GHL #2 - Review & Reputation Management

How it works: After a service is completed, a webhook triggers a satisfaction check. Happy customers (rating 4-5) get a Google/Yelp review request link. Unhappy customers get routed to a private feedback form instead. Follow-up reminders go out at Day 2 and Day 5 for those who haven't left a review yet.

How it helps: Builds your online reputation on autopilot. Filters unhappy customers to private channels (preventing bad public reviews) while funneling happy ones to Google — the #1 factor in local search rankings.

GHL #3 - Appointment Reminder & No-Show Recovery

How it works: Sends reminders at 24 hours and 1 hour before appointments via SMS and email with confirm/reschedule options. If the client no-shows, a recovery sequence kicks in: immediate text, follow-up email next day, and a final rebooking offer with a discount at Day 3.

How it helps: Reduces no-shows by 30-50% through timely reminders. Recovers lost revenue by automatically re-engaging no-shows with rebooking incentives instead of losing them forever.

GHL #4 - Database Reactivation & Win-Back Campaign

How it works: A scheduled trigger runs weekly, pulling contacts who haven't engaged in 90+ days. Sends a "We miss you" email with a special offer, followed by an SMS at Day 3 and a final "last chance" email at Day 7 with an expiring discount code. Tracks opens and re-engagement.

How it helps: Your existing database is your most underused asset. This workflow turns cold leads and past customers into revenue without ad spend — typically generating 5-15% reactivation rates from contacts you already paid to acquire.

Sample Project Automation

GHL #5 - Invoice & Payment Follow-Up

How it works: When an invoice is created (webhook), it sends a professional HTML invoice email + SMS with a pay-now link. If unpaid after 3 days, a friendly reminder goes out. At Day 10, an overdue notice with urgency. Day 14 triggers a final warning. Throughout, the billing team gets alerts on overdue accounts, and all invoices are logged to Excel.

How it helps: Eliminates awkward payment chasing. Businesses typically collect 25-40% faster with automated reminders. The escalating tone (friendly → urgent → final notice) motivates payment without damaging client relationships.
